Brooke's Portfolio

Howdy! I'm Brooke, a young programmer based out of Colorado in the United States. This is a small website written from scratch to help me learn HTML, while showing off some of my hobbies and things i've worked on over the last few years.

Programming

I currently take an AP Computer Science class at a high school in Colorado. I've had a passion for programming since I first tried it at the age of six. Since then, I've done freelance and personal projects my entire life. Usually I modify the code of games I enjoy, which taught me the basics of several different coding languages. This means I have a diverse portfolio and lots of practice learning new software!

Linguistics

As soon as my first day of learning Swedish, I was hooked. I quickly enrolled in a Swedish school and began studying several hours a day. I later discovered Toki Pona, a minimalist constructed language that makes use of an infinite amount of compound phrases to only use around 150 core vocabulary words. Although it only took me around six months to become fluent, it's helped me with mental health and further language learning, two of it's primary goals. Entomology and the evolution of languages continue to fascinate me

Art and Music

I began learning piano at 7, and absolutely hated it, but as I got older and older I slowly began to appreciate creating music more and more. Now I own over a dozen instruments from all over the world! No matter if it's just a few notes on a kalimba, or trying to compose a masterpiece on a lyre, there's few more soothing things to me than creating music. Art comes from a similar vein. I've taken around four years of art classes, and have improved astronomically. Although traditional art's beautiful, I love making three dimensional art also. My mediums of choice are typically clay, wood, or even just cardboard and duct tape! I hope to gain more ways to express myself artistically in the future.
