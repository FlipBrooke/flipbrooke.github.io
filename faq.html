FAQ

Why is your name FlipBrooke?

Brooke is my first name, and a flip book is an animation medium I like. Mash them together and you get FlipBrooke.

What work do you do?

I currently do traditional art and programming. I also research linguistics as a hobby

Will you continue to work on this thing?

I mostly just work on things when I find the time, but there's no harm in asking if it's not marked as discontinued.
